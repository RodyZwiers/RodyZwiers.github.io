Information

The At the beach Festival is the largest beach festival in the Netherlands and the most prestigious
such festival in Central Europe. It is one of the oldest generic festivals (i.e., non-specialized festivals
with a broad divercity of music genres). Among music lovers the At the beach festival is considered the most
diverse family orietnted festival of the Netherlands. Every year, the festival presents 5 main stream bands from
the Netherlands. At the beach is intended for both music lovers, fans and the general public looking for a good time.
